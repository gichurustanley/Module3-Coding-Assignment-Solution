Our chicken dishes are created with juicy, tender cuts and seasoned to perfection. For a classic take on the beloved bird, try our Roasted Chicken, served with creamy mashed potatoes, herb-infused gravy, and roasted vegetables. If you're in the mood for something a little more exotic, our Chicken Teriyaki, served on a bed of steaming white rice and topped with sesame seeds and fresh green onions, is sure to delight your palate. Our succulent beef dishes are the ultimate indulgence for meat lovers. Try our juicy Steak served with sautéed mushrooms, roasted potatoes, and a flavorful herb-butter sauce. Or for something a little more fiery, our Spicy Beef Stir-Fry, tossed with fresh veggies and served with steamed rice, is the perfect blend of spice and savory flavors. Sushi lovers, rejoice! Our menu offers a variety of fresh, handmade sushi options that are perfect for any occasion. Our classic California Roll, filled with crab meat, avocado, and cucumber, is always a crowd pleaser. For something a little more adventurous, try our Spicy Tuna Roll, filled with fresh tuna, fiery sauce, and topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds. Vegetarian options are available as well with our Avocado Roll, filled with fresh avocado, cucumber, and carrots, served with a side of soy sauce.Our hotel menu has been designed to cater to all tastes and preferences.
